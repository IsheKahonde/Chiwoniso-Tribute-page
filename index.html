Chiwoniso Maraire
(5 March 1976 – 24 July 2013)

"Music..Is an expression of God.
All pain, joy, rage, love...
wisdom, can be found in music.
There's a place from where it comes from.
Life essence".
Chiwoniso Maraire

Chi, as she was known as was, one the greatest Zimbabwean musicians.
Popularly known for her mastery in playing the mbirafind more instrument.
She shall forever be missed.

Did You Know?
After failing at several attempts, Chiwoniso never had a driver’s license.
